# 🏟️ StadiumFlow AI



\*\*StadiumFlow AI\*\* is a full-stack, Generative AI-powered platform designed to improve stadium operations and the overall tournament experience during large sporting events such as the FIFA World Cup 2026.



The platform supports fans, volunteers, venue staff, security teams, and organizers by providing intelligent stadium navigation, multilingual assistance, crowd-management insights, accessibility support, transportation guidance, incident reporting, sustainability monitoring, and real-time operational recommendations.



The application includes a working frontend and backend with secure login, role-based dashboards, responsive design, and AI-assisted features.



\---



## 🎯 Purpose of the Application



Large stadium events often face problems such as:



\- Fans getting lost inside the stadium

\- Heavy crowding near gates and facilities

\- Long waiting times

\- Language barriers

\- Difficulty finding accessible routes

\- Transportation confusion after matches

\- Delayed incident reporting

\- Poor communication between staff and volunteers

\- Difficulty making quick operational decisions



StadiumFlow AI brings these services together in one platform and uses Generative AI to provide clear, practical, and context-aware guidance.
